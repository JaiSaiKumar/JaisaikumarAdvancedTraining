Butter-chicken

ingrediants:chicken,spices,tomatoes,cream

Heat a pan with butter & saute cinnamon, cloves and cardamoms for a min.Add ginger garlic paste, green chilies and fry till it turns fragrant.Next pour the tomato cashew puree.Add red chili powder, sugar and salt.Mix and cook until the tomato puree becomes thick."

Onion poha

Ingredients:Poha,peanuts,Onion,Spices

Heat a pan with coconut oil, add chopped onion ,chopped chilli and peanuts, mix well then add spices and fry after add tumeric powder a little then add poha in pan and mix well after some time serve with lemon or curd.

Dal_Khichd

Ingredients:Dal,Onion,Tomatopieces,Rice,Greenchili

Rinse rice and moong dal in a pot for 5 min. chopp onion,tomato. heat a pressure cooker with oil, add cumin seeds, onion and tomato and green chilis, add slices and mix well. Add rice and dal in cooker, pour 4 cups of water and mix, cover pressure cooker, wait until 2-3 whistles open the lid and stir well.

Anda-Curry

Ingredients:Onion,Tomato,spices,Eggs,GingerGarlicPaste,GreenChilli,CurryLeaves

Chop 2 onions. blend tomato in blender jar. take 2 boiled eggs. heat a pan with oil. add spices, onion and tomato paste, mix well.pour 1 cup water and make gravy, add eggs into pan. cook for 5 minute. and serve with roti or naan.

Dosa

Ingredients:Rice,UradDal,ChanaDal,Salt

soak urad dal and chana dal together in water as well for a 4-5 hours..next grind well ,Make it smooth. Add salt to the batter. Leave it at room temperature for or overnight. Heat a pan, Apply oil and spread it evenly with a spatula.then pour it over the surface of pan. cook for 2 minutes from both the sides and serve.

Egg Omelett

ingrediants: Onion,Tomato,GreenChilli,spices,Eggs"

Take a bowl. Break 2 eggs in that bowl,add chopped onion,tomato and chillis. Then add spices and mix well. Add oil to a pan and heat it,after some time pour the eggs mixture. Wait untill the base firms up,Flip it to the other side cook until the eggs are cooked. Switch off the stove to finish off. and serve as it or with the bread
